Our Therapists

Experts Who Care, Innovators Who Lead

At Positivty, our global network of licensed therapists is the heartbeat of our mission. Each professional is rigorously vetted, bringing years of experience and diverse specializations — from cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) to trauma healing and relationship counselling. What sets our therapists apart is their autonomy: they set their own rates, schedules, and therapeutic methods, ensuring every session is as unique as the individual seeking help. Beyond one-on-one care, our therapists are community builders, hosting workshops, co-authoring groundbreaking studies, and leading global mental health initiatives. Whether you’re tackling anxiety, navigating family conflicts, or seeking corporate wellness solutions, our therapists are here to guide you with empathy, expertise, and innovation.
